Google Calendar Integration

Link your Google Calendar to show availability on the booking form. Customers will see when you have open slots.

Setup Instructions

  1. Go to Google Calendar on your computer.
  2. Click the gear icon → Settings.
  3. Under "Settings for my calendars", click on your Aquafresh calendar.
  4. Scroll to "Integrate calendar" and copy the Calendar ID (e.g. abc123@group.calendar.google.com).
  5. For the public embed, scroll to "Access permissions" and check "Make available to public".
  6. Paste your Calendar ID below.
